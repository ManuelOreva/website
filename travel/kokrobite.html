Kokrobite


overview:

Kokrobite is a town along the Atlantic coast. It is a fishing community thirty kilometers (30km) to the west of Accra. The town has budget friendly accommodation and dining options with relaxing reggae vibes.

Kokrobite is an ideal place to spend a few days relaxing while soaking in the view of its tropical beach. It is a popular destination for tourists, backpackers and international volunteers seeking beaches and a break from the busy capital city.

Koklobite is noted for its white-sand beaches and exciting nightlife. African drummers and dancers exhibit impeccable cultural performances at night. On Saturday nights everyone dances beneath the coconut trees to a reggae and highlife band. However, the month of May is the traditional festive season of 'Homowo', during which period noise making and large social events are banned. The waves at Kokrobite make it an ideal place for amateur and professional surfers. Mr Brights surf shop at the beach offers surf lessons and surf board to hire.

  • At about 8 A.M we will arrive at Kokrobite and get a room at Big Millys- A popular pup and hotel at the Kokrobite Beach.
  • Relax on the beach and enjoy a nice meal and drinks.
  • Take a walk on the beach and discover the many African Artworks, clothing, crafts or souvenirs.
  • Watch the colorful fishing boats along the shore.
  • Enjoy tasty organic fruits and meals.
  • Enjoy Reggae Night on Friday Nights and African culture, music and performances from 2 P.M through the night on Saturdays.
