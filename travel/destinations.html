Our Tours

Accra City Tour

Accra is the capital and largest city of Ghana. It is in the Greater Accra Region. The main Ga group known as the ‘Tumgwa We’, arrived by sea. When the Guan (Lartehs) on the coast saw them on their canoes at sea, they looked like ants.

Accra Food Tour

This One day tour takes you on a City tour within Accra. This tour provides a perfect experience to the city life of Accra. You will understand the culture and history of the indigenous people of Accra as well as the cultural diversity of the city and how it impacts on the lives of the people.

Ada Foah

Ada foah is paradise town of the Greater Accra Region of Ghana. It is an hour drive from Accra with many impressive sights. Experience the ambience of Ada Foah by land and boat. Without any doubt Ada Foah is a place of relaxation.

read more

Volta Region

The Volta Region of Ghana is known for its natural attractions and beautiful sights. On a guided tour from Accra, it’s possible to see Volta’s most popular spots in a day. The tour leads you to a monkey sanctuary, Wli waterfall and the high peak of Mount Afadja.

read more

Cape Coast Castle

Cape Coast is a fishing port city situated in the Central Region of Ghana. The arterial language of Cape Coast is Fante. Founded by the people of Oguaa, Cape Coast is one of the most historical cities in Ghana.

read more

Mole National Park

Mole National Park is Ghana's largest national park, situated in the heart of the pristine Guinea Savannah Woodland ecosystem in northern Ghana. It covers an area of 4,577 square kilometres (km²) and provides the best wildlife experience.

read more

Green Ranch

The Green Ranch is an Eco-Friendly Equestrian Leisure Center located in the Ashanti Region of Ghana just about 40 km from Kumasi. The Green Ranch is a horse-back riding destination overlooking beautiful Lake Bosomtwe.

read more

Ashanti Region

The Ashanti Region is the 3rd largest region in the country, and centrally located, with an area of about 24,389 km2. This region is inhabited by the Ashantis. It is one of the most important regions, and second to the capital city Accra, economically and politically.

read more

Kokrobite

Kokrobite is a town along the Atlantic coast. It is a fishing community thirty kilometers (30km) to the west of Accra. The town has budget friendly accommodation and dining options with relaxing reggae vibes.

read more

Nzulezo

Nzulezo is a village built entirely on stilts on Amanzule river. It is constructed out of wood and raffia with one central walkway and to dozen houses on either side. The only way to get there is to travel 5km on the lake in a local canoe.

read more

Busia Beach

Busia beach is about 7 hours drive from Accra and located in the Western Region of Ghana. It is one of the cleanest beaches in Ghana with low tides. This makes it an ideal place for amateur surfers and swimming.
