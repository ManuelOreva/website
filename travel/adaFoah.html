Ada Foah


overview:

Ada foah is paradise town of the Greater Accra Region of Ghana. It is an hour drive from Accra with many impressive sights. Experience the ambience of Ada Foah by land and boat. Without any doubt Ada Foah is a place of relaxation. Here, you will get in touch with nature and your inner self. People who come to Ada Foah come to happiness and leave with joy in their heart.

Among the many attractions are the Ada Foah Estruary, Aqua Safari, Crocodile Islands, mangrove forest, Bird sighting and Clean Long Sunny Beaches, Beach Soccer and Cultural Performances.

schedule:

Estuary: We will take a boat to the estuary where the largest man- made lake meets the South Atlantic Ocean. Get closer. Watch these two different colored waters struggle with each other but never mixing together. Duration: 1:30 hours

Crocodile Island: From here we move to see the Crocodile Island. To be a true friend to the crocs, we offer them a live chicken. Take pictures with the Crocodiles after the generous offer. Duration: 2:30 hours

We move into Mangrove forest on Aflive Island, discover local animal species in their habitat and weave local mats/baskets. On the same Island taste local gin and package some for your trip. Duration 1 hour

Songor salt lagoon: Experience and learn how the salt is locally mined and processed in Ghana at the Songor Salt Lagoon. Working hours: Monday to Friday 9:30am to 4:00pm

Old fort police station and sacred grove: Visit the old fort police station to learn the history about slavery in Ghana. Duration: 1 Hour

Turtle watching tour (seasonal):Join one of the trained tour guides on a nightly turtle watching tour and experience one of the three different species of marine turtles coming to the beach to lay eggs. Tours take place from the month of September to February. Duration: 2:30 Hour

Asafotufiami festival tour (seasonal):Experience Ada in its celebration mood during the Asafotufiami festival. Locals and tourists come together to experience ancient history, following a full seven-days-schedule of amazing cultural display. Asafotufiami festival starts every first Thursday of August and lasts for one week. Tours are on Asafotu-Saturday from 8am to 2pm
