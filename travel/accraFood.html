Accra Food Tour


overview:

This One day tour takes you on a City tour within Accra. This tour provides a perfect experience to the city life of Accra. You will understand the culture and history of the indigenous people of Accra as well as the cultural diversity of the city and how it impacts on the lives of the people.

schedule:

Your tour Guide and Driver will arrive at the hotel at 7:30am. At 8am we should be set to start.

This trip will take your through various places of Accra to have Break Fast, Lunch and Supper. Keep your stomach empty to have a taste of Ghanaian dishes and beverages from morning to evening.

During the tour you will stop at various restaurants to have a taste of the various dishes.

A few of the dishes and beverages to expect:

  • Hausa Koko
  • Waakye
  • Jollof Rice
  • Rice balls with peanut soup
  • Fufu with peanut, palm nut soup, light soup
  • Banku and okra stew or any other soup of choice
  • Banku with Tilapia and cayenne pepper
  • Tuo Zaafi with green leaf stew or soup
  • Red Red (beans and ripe plantain)
  • Sobolo Drink
  • Asana Drink
  • Zowey Adaakwa (Made from peanut and spices)
  • Brukina
  • Bofrot
