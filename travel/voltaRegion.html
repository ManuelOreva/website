Volta Region


overview:

The Volta Region of Ghana is known for its natural attractions and beautiful sights. On a guided tour from Accra, it’s possible to see Volta’s most popular spots in a day. The tour leads you to a monkey sanctuary, Wli waterfall and the high peak of Mount Afadja.

schedule:

Day 1: Mountain Afadjato

  • We will start our adventure from Mountain Afadjato. Climb to the highest point in Ghana. This takes a couple of hours and requires determination and the zeal to achieve. Remember to come along with a water bottle. Duration: 5 hours Included: 1 night accommodation in Ho

Day 2: Wli Waterfalls, Hohoe, Volta Region

  • Visit the Wli waterfall. We will climb the cliffs. A large colony of bats can be seen clinging to the cliffs. We have the liberty to swim at the fall. Duration: 3 hours

Day 3: Akosombo Hydro Plant, Akosombo, Eastern Region

  • The Akosombo Dam, also known as the Volta Dam, is a hydroelectric dam on the Volta River in south-eastern Ghana. The construction of the dam flooded part of the Volta River Basin, and led to the subsequent creation of Lake Volta. We will take a canoe ride on Lake Volta (largest man-made lake in Ghana). A canoe ride on the lake is one of the most beautiful sights in Ghana. Duration: 3 hours Included: 1 night accommodation at Akosombo hotel

Day 4: Tafi Atome Monkey Sanctuary,Tafi Atome, Volta Region

  • We make our way to the Tafi Atome monkey sanctuary. You will get up close to the monkeys and feed them. From the Monkey santuary we have a relaxing day at the Keta lagoon or one of the many beaches in the Volta region.
