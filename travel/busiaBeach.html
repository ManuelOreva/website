Busia Beach


overview:

Busia beach is about 7 hours drive from Accra and located in the Western Region of Ghana. It is one of the cleanest beaches in Ghana with low tides. This makes it an ideal place for amateur surfers and swimming. Enjoy your stay in beautiful hotels facing the blue water beach. The sight at Busua beach is a marvel to the eye. Even the blind can enjoy the soothing sounds of the sea waves. To enjoy the native festival (Asa Baako) of the people of Busia, visit from the first to 6th days of March.

schedule:

  • At 6:00 A.M your tour guide, will pick you up at your hotel.
  • It's about 7 hour drive from Accra to Takoradi and hour drive from Takoradi to Busia beach.
  • Mr Bright Surf Shop and School at the beach are open to aspiring surfers and professional surfers. Surfing in these clean blue waters is an experience of a lifetime.

important:

  • Hydration BackPacks
  • Water bottles
  • Mosquitoe Repellant
  • Sunscreen
  • Malaria Pills

It would help to have a travel Insurance package.
