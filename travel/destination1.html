Accra City Tour


overview:

This One day tour takes you on a City tour within Accra. It provides a perfect experience to the city life of Accra. You will understand the culture and history of the indigenous people of Accra as well as the cultural diversity of the city and how it impacts on the lives of the people.

schedule:

  • Day 1

    Kotoka International Airport

    You will be met at the Kotoka International Airport and driven to your hotel. Depending on your time of arrival in Ghana you will be taken on a 1 hour drive through some interesting places around your hotel during which period you will have your orientation.

    Accommodation included

  • Day 2

    • Your tour Guide and Driver will arrive at the hotel at 7:30am. At 8am we should be set to start.

    You will start your tour with a visit to the W.E.B DuBois Centre in Accra.

    Our next stop will be to the Black Star Square also known as the Independence Square. It was built by Ghana’s first president Dr Kwame Nkrumah to celebrate Ghana's independence.

    From the Black Star Square you will visit the Center for National Culture known as the Art Center where you can have free drum lesson and visit the African Market. Duration: 1 hour

    Our next stop is at the Kwame Nkrumah Memorial Park, Accra. This is the memorial site of Ghana’s first president and the African of the 20th century, Osagyefo Dr Kwame Nkrumah. Duration: 1 hour

    You will continue your tour by visiting the famous Jamestown lighthouse fishing Village. Duration: 1 hour

    You will end your tour for the day with a trip to Labadi Beach to experience the white sandy beach with cool breeze and Ghanaian beverages and kebab. Duration: 3 hours
