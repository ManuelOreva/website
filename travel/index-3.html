Accra City Tour

Accra is the capital and largest city of Ghana. It is in the Greater Accra Region. The main Ga group known as the ‘Tumgwa We’, arrived by sea. When the Guan (Lartehs) on the coast saw them on their canoes at sea, they looked like ants. Hence, the Lartehs refer to them as Nkran (ants). Nkran was later corrupted by the Danes to Akra, then to present-day Accra. Nkran in the Ga language is Gaga, thus they also started calling themselves Ga. The indigenous Lartehs thus relocated to the Akuapem ridge.

Ada Foah

Ada foah is paradise town of the Greater Accra Region of Ghana. It is an hour drive from Accra with many impressive sights. Experience the ambience of Ada Foah by land and boat. Without any doubt Ada Foah is a place of relaxation. Here, you will get in touch with nature and your inner self. People who come to Ada Foah come to happiness and leave with joy in their heart. Among the many attractions are the Ada Foah Estruary, Aqua Safari, Crocodile Islands, mangrove forest, Bird sighting and Clean Long Sunny Beaches, Beach Soccer and Cultural Performances.

Cape Coast Castle

Cape Coast is a fishing port city situated in the Central Region of Ghana. The arterial language of Cape Coast is Fante. Founded by the people of Oguaa, Cape Coast is one of the most historical cities in Ghana. From the 16th century until Ghanaian independence, the city and fishing port changed hands between the British, the Portuguese, the Swedish, the Danish and the Dutch. It is enriched with thirty two (32) Festivals. The Cape Coast Castle which is now a World Heritage Site is a testament to European visits to this land of splendor.
